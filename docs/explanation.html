Slay the Text — How to Play

Audience: Brand-new players. If you have never played Slay the Spire (or its text version), start here. No prior knowledge assumed.

What is this game?

Slay the Text is a single-player, turn-based deck-building roguelike. You pick one of three heroes and climb a tower called The Spire. Along the way you fight monsters with a deck of cards, collect relics and potions, and try to reach the top without dying. When you die, the run is over — that is the "roguelike" part.

How you control the game

Everything is text. In almost every situation the game shows you a numbered list and you type the number of what you want, then press Enter.

  • Don't know what something does? Type its exact name (e.g. Strike, Burning Blood) at most prompts to get a short explanation.
  • You can save only during a battle: type Save and press Enter.
  • The very first time you launch, say Yes to the built-in tutorial — it covers the same basics live.

The map

Each Act is a branching map. You pick which connected room to enter next:

RoomWhat happens
CreepA normal fight.
EliteA harder fight that always drops a Relic.
Event (?)A story choice — could be good or risky.
Shop ($)Spend gold on cards, relics, potions, or card removal.
Fires (Rest Site)Heal, or upgrade a card.
ChestA free relic.
BossThe Act boss. Beat it to advance.

A turn of combat, step by step

  1. You gain Energy (3 by default) and draw 5 cards.
  2. Each card costs Energy. Attacks deal damage, Skills defend or help, Powers are permanent buffs for the rest of the fight.
  3. Look at each enemy's Intent — it tells you what they will do next turn — and decide whether to attack or defend.
  4. Block soaks up incoming damage but disappears at the start of your next turn, so spend it the turn you make it.
  5. End your turn. Leftover cards are discarded; enemies act; repeat until someone wins.

Words you'll see a lot

VulnerableTarget takes 50% more attack damage.
WeakTarget deals 25% less attack damage.
FrailTarget gains 25% less Block.
PoisonLose that much HP each turn; the number then drops by 1.
Strength+damage on each attack hit.
Dexterity+Block from each Block card.
ExhaustCard leaves the deck for the rest of this combat.

Your first goals

  1. Stay alive. HP carries between fights and is hard to get back.
  2. Keep your deck small. Remove weak starter cards at shops so you draw your good cards more often.
  3. Block the big hits, attack the small ones. Read intents and react.
  4. Don't hoard potions. A potion that saves your run is worth more than one you never used.

When this feels comfortable, read General Tips.
