General Tips

Audience: Players who know the rules and have finished a fight or two, but lose runs and aren't sure why. These habits win the most games for the least effort.

Deckbuilding

  • Smaller decks are stronger. Every card you add dilutes your draws. Remove Strikes and Defends at shops once you have better cards.
  • Don't take every card offered. Skipping a card reward is often correct — a bloated deck of mediocre cards loses to a lean deck with a plan.
  • Have an answer to damage. A deck that only attacks dies to elites and bosses. Carry enough Block/defence to survive their big intents.
  • Upgrade your most-played cards at Rest Sites — your cheap attacks and your block card usually matter more than a flashy rare.

Combat habits

  • Read every intent before you act. Block the turns they hit hard; dump damage the turns they buff or do nothing.
  • Apply Vulnerable before your big attack — 50% more damage is enormous on your highest-hitting card.
  • Don't over-block. Block not used this turn is wasted; if an enemy hits for 8, you rarely need 20 Block.
  • Front-load multi-hit fights. Killing one enemy fast removes its whole damage output for the rest of the fight.

Map routing

  • Plan your path to Rest Sites and the Elite reward you want. You usually can't hit every room — choose the route, don't wander.
  • Fight Elites when your deck is ready — the free relic is worth it, but a bad early elite can end a run.
  • "?" Events are a gamble. Read them; many trade HP or a curse for a powerful reward.
  • Rest = heal early, upgrade late. Heal when low; upgrade when safe.

Economy

  • Card removal is one of the best uses of gold. Thinning your deck pays off every fight.
  • Buy potions before a known boss. Don't end a run with three unused potions.
  • Relics are forever; potions are once. Prioritise relics that scale with your deck.

Character notes

  • Silent: Either go wide on Poison (stack it, then it does the work) or fast with Shivs/discard. Don't mix half-heartedly.
  • Ironclad: Strength + a way to attack repeatedly, plus block and self-heal, is the reliable plan.
  • Defect: Decide between Frost (defence) and Lightning (offence), and pick up Focus to make every orb better.
